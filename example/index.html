RAWR!

Make Your Design Scream

RAWR! can be thought of as a framework to help developers design websites. It was developed during the creation of Hacker League by me, Ian Jennings. It is a full implementation of ideas presented in this slideshow by Johan Ronsse bundled with a few extras to help get you designing quickly.

You'll probably be interested in RAWR! if:

  • You would prefer using an IDE rather than opening Photoshop or Illustrator #44
  • You write scripts to solve problems #44
  • You want your design to look passable without the intervention of a dedicated designer #43
